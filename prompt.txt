Below is a **master frontend prompt** optimized for AI website builders like **Antigravity**, **Lovable**, **Bolt.new**, **v0**, or similar tools. It is written as a product requirements document (PRD), which generally produces better results than a short prompt.

# MASTER FRONTEND PROMPT

## Project Title

**Student Biodata Portal for Rauda College of Health Science and Technology**

---

## Role

Act as a **Senior UI/UX Designer and Senior Frontend Engineer**.

Design and build a **modern, responsive, production-ready frontend** for a **Student Biodata Portal**.

This is **NOT** an admission portal.

This is **NOT** a student management system.

This is specifically a **Student Biodata Portal** where students complete their biodata after creating an account.

Design it like a modern SaaS dashboard with clean UI, smooth animations, accessibility, and responsive layouts.

# User Roles

Only three roles exist.

## Student

Can:

* Register
* Login
* Complete biodata
* Upload passport photograph
* View submission status
* Download biodata PDF

---

## Reviewer

Can:

* Login
* View submissions
* Search students
* Review biodata
* Approve
* Reject
* Add review comments
* Export students (CSV and PDF)

---

## Super Admin

Can:

* Login
* View dashboard
* Receive notifications of newly registered students
* View all submissions
* Manage reviewer accounts
* Generate reports

Super Admin does NOT review biodata.

# Validation Rules

Student CANNOT submit unless

English Language

≥ Credit

AND

Mathematics

≥ Credit

AND

Minimum 5 Credits.

Show live validation.

Green

```
Eligible

✔ You meet the minimum requirement.
```

Red

```
Not Eligible

Minimum 5 credits required.

English and Mathematics must both have credit passes.
```

Disable Submit button until validation passes.
